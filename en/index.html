The goals, objectives and functions of the National Bank

Main

The main goal of the National Bank of the Kyrgyz Republics are the achievement and maintenance of price stability, by conducting the appropriate monetary politicians.

The main task that contributes to the achievement of the goal of activity bank is to maintain purchasing power national currency, ensuring security and reliability banking and payment systems of the republic.
